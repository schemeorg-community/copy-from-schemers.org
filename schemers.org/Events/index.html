Past Events

The annual Scheme and Functional Programming workshop series has its own home page.

  • The 2009 Scheme workshop was in Boston.
  • ICFP 2008 featured the Scheme 2008 workshop.
  • ICFP 2007 featured the Scheme 2007 workshop.
  • ICFP 2006 featured the Scheme 2006 workshop.

  • ICFP 2005 featured the Scheme 2005 workshop.
  • ECOOP 2005 featured the 2nd European LISP and Scheme Workshop.
  • Olin Shivers and Oscar Waddell chaired the 2004 Scheme Workshop in Snowbird, Utah, in September, 2004. The workshop's Web site includes the proceedings.
  • Pascal Costanza organized the First European Lisp and Scheme Workshop in conjunction with ECOOP in Oslo in June, 2004. The workshop's Web site includes the proceedings.
  • Matthew Flatt chaired the 2003 Scheme Workshop in Boston in November, 2002. Jay McCarthy took detailed notes from the event.
  • Olin Shivers chaired Scheme and Functional Programming, 2002 in Pittsburgh in October, 2002. The workshop's Web site includes the proceedings.
  • Manuel Serrano chaired Scheme and Functional Programming, 2001 in Florence in September, 2001. The workshop's Web site includes the proceedings.
  • Matthias Felleisen organized a workshop, Scheme and Functional Programming, as part of Principles, Logics, and Implementations of High-Level Programming Languages (PLI) in September 2000. The workshop's Web site includes the proceedings.
  • Richard Kelsey organized a Scheme Strawman Workshop in conjunction with the International Conference on Functional Programming (ICFP '98) in September, 1998. The minutes are on-line.
  • Functional Languages in the Introductory Computing Curriculum (held on May 24, 1996)
  • Scheme Implementors' Workshop (held on May 23, 1996)
  • 20th Anniversary Scheme Workshop (held on January 21, 1996)
